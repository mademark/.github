**An open authorship standard for AI-era content.**

## Three Marks. Three Methods.

**[m]** Human Made — Conceived and produced by people  
**[m/a]** Human Designed, AI Made — Human creative direction, AI production  
**[a]** AI Made — Conceived and produced primarily by AI

## About

Made Mark is an open specification for content authorship disclosure. It answers the question no existing standard addresses: how was this content made?

The specification is public domain (CC0). The mark icons are openly licensed (CC BY-ND 4.0). The name and logo are trademarked to protect signal integrity.
